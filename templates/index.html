About

I've made this website as somewhere between a blog, a CV and a general homepage.

Forgive the self-promotion! I built this to serve as a useful place to document experiences, as well as share some parts of my life through the blog and photography sections.

I moved to Amsterdam in October '24 from my native UK. I figured that this might be a good exercise to test some of my skills in the cloud and create something useful to share with folk that I meet.

In the Blog, I'll be sharing updates and thoughts. I've tried to keep diaries in the past, and hopefully this will be more successful.

The Photography section is for some of the photos that I'm proud of from the last few years and onward. My sisters got me into film photography, and I've been annoying my friends with it ever since.

In the Professional section I want to share some of my projects, qualifications, and experiences. Hopefully this is a platform where I can talk a bit more in-depth about things that doesn't come across from LinkedIn and other places - I'll try to keep it as interesting and light as possible...

I've shared my public email address, so don't hesitate to get in touch based on anything you see on this site. I'm always happy to respond to anything that's not creepy or weird!
